PROGRAMMES WE ARE OFFERING

MBA

If you’re interested in earning a Master’s in Business Administration (MBA) but need flexibility, our self-study-based online MBA program is ideal. Study core business concepts like finance, marketing, leadership, and entrepreneurship at your own pace — from anywhere in the world, across 32 specialized areas.

Whether you want to launch a startup, step into leadership, or change industries, this MBA helps get you there. Designed specifically for working professionals, it connects study materials with real-world work experience — and boosts both your resume and earning potential.

Master’s

Practitioners Doctorate

Advance your career with a research-focused Practitioners Doctorate — completely online and tailored for professionals seeking academic credibility without compromising work-life balance.

Your previous research contributions (like published articles, books, or chapters) can serve as your thesis and be presented for external review and defense.

This program is ideal for professionals in education, healthcare, social work, and more — helping you create real-world impact through scholarly achievement.

Honorary Doctorate for Scholars

The Honorary Doctorate is awarded to outstanding professionals who have made impactful contributions through published work, innovation, or service to their field. This flexible, fully online route allows accomplished individuals to be recognized without interrupting their careers.

Applicants present existing scholarly work — such as publications, books, or journal articles — which is reviewed and defended before an academic panel of examiners.

Whether you're a leader in education, healthcare, business, or social science, this prestigious credential highlights your achievements and enhances your global professional recognition.
