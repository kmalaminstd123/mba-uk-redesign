World-Class Master's Program

The World Academy Masters program is a prestigious educational opportunity, offering participants the chance to earn a Master's degree from a European International University (EIU-Paris). The program emphasizes global perspectives, preparing graduates to succeed in careers that require an understanding of cultural diversity and international cooperation. Specialization covers a range of areas, from HR, Project Management, Supply Chain, Strategy, Leadership and disaster and Risk Management. Graduates of the program are highly sought after by employers across a variety of industries and sectors.

Awarding Body of This Program European International University (EIU-Paris)

World Academy is the global partner of European International University (EIU-Paris), which is globally acknowledged for its academic excellence and ensures that participants are provided with a conducive learning experience that fosters knowledge acquisition and skills development. EIU-Paris is ranked 51 in 2024 and 55 among all the business schools and universities in 2023 according to the CEO world Magazine. In addition, EIU-Paris has a QS STAR rating of 5 stars for online learning and teaching quality.

Application & Admission procedure at a Glance

1

Select Your Program

Select any Master's from our wide variety of Specialization and submit the required documents via email to apply.

2

Eligibility Check

The required documents submitted by candidates will be assessed by the Admission Committee to determine eligibility for the program along with the partial scholarship.

3

Confirmation of Eligibility

An email confirmation will be sent regarding the decision of the admission committee.

4

Get Offer Letter

Selected Candidates will receive an offer letter for the program applied, along with the invoice for the first payment.

5

Make Payment

Pay the invoice amount and wait for the confirmation. Your profile will be activated on the portal within 24hrs.

6

Receive Acceptance Letter

From the day of payment, participants will get the Acceptance Letter within 7-10 working days.

7

Academic Orientation

The Virtual orientation will be conducted within 5 to 7 working days of enrollment completion.

8

Start the Course

After orientation, begin your program with full support from our team for any assistance needed.

9

Graduation Requirements

Complete all ECT credits, pass assignments and ARP with minimum 2.5 GPA, and fulfill financial obligations.

10

Certificate

Receive official Certificate and Transcript (Electronic and Printed version) upon successful program completion.

11

Convocation

Optional attendance at convocation ceremony with separate registration required for graduates.

Program Benefits

Supply Chain Management

A MSSCM degree prepares participants for leadership roles in managing production and logistics processes. Through courses on procurement, operations management, and logistics, participant learn to optimize the flow of goods and services from suppliers to consumers. With Supply Chain Management becoming increasingly important in today's interconnected global economy, a Masters of Science in Supply Chain Management degree is a valuable asset for professionals seeking to advance their careers.

Human Resource Management

MSHRM specialization focuses on developing individuals who can effectively manage organizational talent in a rapidly changing global environment. Graduates will have the skills and knowledge needed to lead HR departments, develop and implement policies and training programs, and ensure compliance with relevant laws and regulations. This degree can lead to a variety of careers in human resources, including HR manager, recruiter, compensation and benefits specialist, and employee relations specialist.

Entry Requirements

  • Recognized Bachelor Degree in any field
  • At least 1 years of working experience in the managerial position
