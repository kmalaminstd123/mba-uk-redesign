World-Class MBA Program

World Academy is offering the MBA program which is designed and provided to professionals who wants to equip themselves with skills, knowledge and experience needed to succeed in the global business environment. We aim in develop business leaders with a strategic perspective, analytical skills, and an entrepreneurial mindset. The application-based curriculum will teach participants to think critically, manage complex projects, and communicate effectively with diverse stakeholders. Graduates of the program are prepared to take on leadership roles in a range of industries and sectors, from finance and consulting to technology and healthcare.

Awarding Body of This Program European International University (EIU-Paris)

World Academy is the global partner of European International University (EIU-Paris), which is globally acknowledged for its academic excellence and ensures that participants are provided with a conducive learning experience that fosters knowledge acquisition and skills development. EIU-Paris is ranked 55 among all the universities in 2023 according to the CEO world Magazine. In addition, EIU-Paris has a QS STAR rating of 5 stars for online learning and teaching quality and its graduates can obtain US and Canadian equivalency for their degrees. EIU-Paris graduates also have direct access to UK dual awards/qualifications.

Program Goals

The M.B.A. program structure enables delegates to acquire the following knowledge and transferable skills:

  • Essential & modern management skills for playing role as managerial level
  • Adequate financial knowledge to play role in mid to senior level position
  • Essential knowledge on business acumen and its application for business analysis
  • Advanced supervisory and leadership skill to fit in the 21st century leaders’ filed
  • Essential Competency to deal with business crisis in the post Covid world
  • Essential Cost Management Skills for ensuring healthy bottom line
  • Capability to innovative idea and strengthen business expansion through these
  • Self-analysis, self-management and to Emotionally intelligence
  • Expertise on analytical thinking and reasoning skills
  • Critical thinking and problem-solving skills
  • Highly effective communication skills.

Application & Admission Procedure

Your journey to academic excellence in 11 simple steps

1

Select Your Program

Choose from our wide variety of MBA specializations and submit required documents via email.

2

Eligibility Check

Assessment by Admission Committee for program eligibility and scholarship opportunities.

3

Confirmation of Eligibility

An email confirmation will be sent regarding the decision of the admission committee.

4

Get Offer Letter

Selected Candidates will receive an offer letter for the program applied, along with the invoice for the first payment.

5

Make Payment

Pay the invoice amount and wait for the confirmation. Your profile will be activated on the portal within 24hrs.

6

Receive Acceptance Letter

From the day of payment, participants will get the Acceptance Letter within 7-10 working days.

7

Academic Orientation

The Virtual orientation will be conducted within 5 to 7 working days of enrollment completion.

8

Start the Course

After orientation, begin your program with full support from our team for any assistance needed.

9

Graduation Requirements

Complete all ECT credits, pass assignments and ARP with minimum 2.5 GPA, and fulfill financial obligations.

10

Certificate

Receive official Certificate and Transcript (Electronic and Printed version) upon successful program completion.

11

Convocation

Optional attendance at convocation ceremony with separate registration required for graduates.
